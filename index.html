About

This animation project is about getting to know talented animators, artists and interviewing them about their work and what is behind their workflow. This series will be in a season format. So there will be a max number of episodes per season. At the moment the max number of episodes in a season that I have set is 7, these episodes will be spread through the year. Preferably I will be able to get one done every 2 weeks, once all the assets have been created.This series is targeted at young aspiring animators who want to learn about different ways of animation, and hopefully discover what kind of animation or art form they're interested in whether its 2D animation, 3D animation, Puppet animation, 3D background design, 2D background design, Character design, 3D modelling, 3D rigging, 2D rigging and more...
